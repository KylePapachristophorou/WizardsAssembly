Magic The Gathering

Magic The Gathering Was First Released In 1993, There Are 6 types of cards in Magic:

Land: These Are Tapped For Mana, This Mana Is Then Used To Cast Spells.
(Every Card Is A Spell Except Land When It Is First Cast)

Creature Spells: Creature Spells Are Used To Damage Your Opponent,
When They First Enter The Battlefield They Cant Attack For The First Turn Due To Summoning Sickness.
Once Cast These Spells Are Only Sent To The Graveyard By Card Effect
Or When The Creature Is Dealt Damage Equal To Or Higher Then Its Toughness

Sorcery Spells: Sorcery Spells Can Only Be Cast During The Controllers Main Phase Or Main Phase 2,
Once Cast These Spells Are Sent To The Graveyard Unless Otherwise Stated.

Instant Spells: Unlike Sorcery Spells, Instant Spells Can Be Cast At Any Point During either Players Turn.
Instant Spells Can Be Used At The Same Time As Other Instant Spells. (These Resolve On The Stack)
Once Cast These Spells Are Sent To The Graveyard Unless Otherwise Stated.

Enchantment: Enchantments Are Spells That Can Only Only Be Cast During The Controllers Turn,
These Stay On The Battlefield Until Destroyed By The Opponent.
(Note: Spells With The Flash Can Still Be Cast Like Instants.)

Planeswalkers: Planeswalkers Are Spells That Summon A Powerful Ally To Your Aid,
Planeswalkers Have Loyalty Which Acts As A Life Counter For The Card.
Planeswalkers have Abilities That Gain Or Spend That Resource,
They Can Be Targeted By The Opponent For Attacks.
