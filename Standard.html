Standard

Standard Is The Most Widely Played Format,
The Format Is Restricted To Only The Most Recent Sets.
At The Current Time The Legal Sets Are: Kaladesh, Aether Revolt, Amonkhet, Hour Of Devastation And Ixalan.

Players Start With 20 Life Each And 7 Cards, Players Are Allowed To Mulligan.
(Reshuffle And Draw 1 Less Card, Look At The Top Card Of The Deck And Then Choose To Keep It Top Or Place It to The Bottom Of The Library.)

Cards From Older Sets Reprinted In Current Standard Legal Sets Can Be Used,
However Any Text Change Must Be Stated To The Opponent At The Time Of Casting.
