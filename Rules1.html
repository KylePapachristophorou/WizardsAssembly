Rules Of Magic The Gathering

When A Card Is Used It Is Placed SideWays, This Is Called "Tapping" The Card.

Each Player Starts Of with 20 Life, when This Hits 0 That Player

When A Creature Is Summoned, It Has Summoning Sickness Stopping It From Attacking Or Using Mana Abilities.

Each Player Can Only Play 1 Land Card Per Turn

When The Game Starts, Each Player Draws 7 cards.

If The 7 Cards Are Unwanted, The Player Can Muligan.
This Is When The Player Shuffles And Redraws One Less Card,
Then Scrys 1. (Looks At The Top Card, Place It On Top Or Bottom Of Library.)

The Stack Is A Zone Where Spells And Abilities Are Put Where They Wait To Resolve,
The Stack Resolves From Top To Bottom.

Turn Structure
Untap->Upkeep->Draw->Main Phase 1->Combat->Main Phase 2->End Step

The Combat Step Is Completed In The Order Of:
Enter Combat->Declare Attackers->Declare Blockers->First/Double Strike Damage->Combat Damage->End Of Combat
