Islands (Blue Mana)

Blue Cards Generally Follow The Theme Of Knowledge And Deceit,
Common Blue Mechanics Include Spells That Counter Opponents Spells And Creatures.
Other Mechanics Include Creatures That Are Able To Be Played At Instant Speed,
Often With Abilities Such As Flash, Flying And Hexproof.
