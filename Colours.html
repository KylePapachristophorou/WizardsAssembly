Colours

Every Card In Magic The Gathering Is Divided Into Groups Named After Its Casting Cost, An Example Of This Is Lightning Bolt A Single Red Mana To Cast And Is The Most Famous Red Spell In The Game Dealing 3 Damage At Instant Speed To Target Creature Or Player.

Click On The Mana Symbols Below To Learn More About Each Colour, This Will Introduce Many Different New Mechanics.

Blue

Blue's Mana Source Comes From Islands, Often Associated With Control Spells And Flying Creatures.

Red

Red's Mana Source Comes From Mountains, Often Associated With Fire Spells And Creatures That Move Quickly With Haste And Swarm The Battlefield.

Green

Green's Mana Source Comes From Forests, Often Associated With Power Up Spells And Creatures With High Stats And Mana Costs.

Black

Black's Mana Source Comes From Swamps, Often Associated With Death Spells And Curses. Its Famous Archtypes Include Zombies And Vampires.

White

Whites's Mana Source Comes From Plains, Often Associated With Life Gain and Protection Spells. Its Famous Archtypes Include Angels And Control.

Alternative Mana

Some Cards Have Alternate Mana Costs Such As Paying Life Instead Of Mana Or Giving Options On How Much Mana To Spend.
