Insight Into Ixalan

Article Of The Week

With Ixalan Hitting Stores, The Perfect Chance Arises To Inspect The New Cards Hitting The Formats.

With The Tribal Themes Of Pirates, Merfolk, Vampires and Dinosaurs Smashing Into The Meta Standard Is Sure To See New And Interesting Decks Being Used At All Local Friday Night Magic Events.

Famous Cards Such As Metalic Mimic Will Thrive In The New Tribal Based Deck Styles, Especially Merfolk With The New Herald Of The Streams Card Making All Merfolk Unblockable When The Mimic Is Not Taken Care Of Early.

White Receives A Power Up With The New Settle The Wreakage And Ixalans Binding Coupling Up With Cards Already Released Such As Cast Out To Offer Many Ways To Control The Battlefield And Exile Any Troublesome Opponents.

With The Pro Tours Fast Arriving Its An Incredible Time To Be Playing Magic!
The Pros Will Definitely Reveal The Best Decks!
